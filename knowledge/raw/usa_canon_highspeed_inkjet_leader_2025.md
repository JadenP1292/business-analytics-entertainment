# Canon Named Leader in IDC MarketScape: Worldwide High-Speed Inkjet 2025

**Source:** Canon U.S.A. Newsroom
**URL:** https://www.usa.canon.com/newsroom/2026/20260409-recognition
**Date:** April 9, 2026

Canon U.S.A. announced that Canon Inc. has been named a Leader in the IDC MarketScape: Worldwide High-Speed Inkjet 2025 Vendor Assessment (doc #US52990625, December 2025).

## IDC MarketScape Findings

- "Canon maintains a strong presence in the market, with continued growth in new installations and page volume."
- "Since the last iteration of this study, in which Canon was already in the Leaders category, Canon has made significant strides to bolster its position in the segment."

## Key Improvements Cited

- Extended capabilities of established high-speed inkjet products
- Introduced own 1,200 dpi piezo inkjet printhead platform and products
- Announced innovations in service execution for greater end-user control and high uptime
- Expanded finishing solutions through industry partnerships

## Technology Differentiators

Canon's inkjet presses use proprietary printhead technology and innovative ink systems:
- Water-based polymer inks, 1,200 dpi printheads, proprietary waveforms
- Offset-level quality, color consistency, and substrate versatility
- Inline sensors for color profiling and automated maintenance

## High-Speed Inkjet Portfolio

Canon's portfolio spans continuous feed and sheetfed technologies:
- **Sheetfed:** varioPRINT iX1700, iX3200 (B3-format); varioPRESS iV7 (B2-format)
- **Continuous Feed:** ProStream 2000/3000, ColorStream 8000
- **Label/Packaging:** LabelStream LS2000, corrPRESS iB17

Canon combines its own inks, printheads, paper feed, drying/fixing technologies enabling a variety of media in the varioPRINT iX1700 press.

## Strategic Significance

Canon's high-speed inkjet leadership directly competes with HP's Indigo and PageWide divisions in the production print market. Being named Leader twice consecutively signals sustained competitive strength in a segment where HPQ and XRX both compete.
