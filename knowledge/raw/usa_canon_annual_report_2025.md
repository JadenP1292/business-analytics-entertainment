# Canon Publishes Annual Report for the Year Ended Dec. 31, 2025

**Source:** Canon U.S.A. Newsroom
**URL:** https://www.usa.canon.com/newsroom/2026/20260408-annual-report
**Date:** April 8, 2026

Canon Inc., the parent company of Canon U.S.A., Inc., announced publication of the Annual Report 2025 — a consolidated financial report summarizing financial results for fiscal year 2025.

## Key Financial Context

- **Global Revenue:** Approximately $29.5 billion (as of 2025)
- **Patent Leadership:** Ranked in the top-10 for U.S. patents granted for 42 consecutive years (compiled by IFI CLAIMS Patent Services)
- Annual report available at: global.canon/en/ir/library/annual.html
- Message from Chairman and CEO Fujio Mitarai published alongside the report

## Strategic Significance

The annual report confirms Canon's continued financial scale and technology leadership heading into 2026. Canon's $29.5B revenue base provides substantial resources for R&D investment and market expansion — a key competitive advantage versus peers like XRX ($0.24B market cap) and Nikon (NINOY), which are significantly smaller in scale. Canon's 42-year patent track record signals deep technology differentiation in imaging and printing markets.
