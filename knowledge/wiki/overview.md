# Canon Inc. — Overview

*Synthesized from scraped sources in knowledge/raw/. Last updated: 2026-04-22.*

## Who Canon Is

Canon Inc. is a Japanese multinational corporation headquartered in Tokyo, founded in 1937. It is a global leader in imaging technologies, document solutions, and industrial equipment. Canon is publicly listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange and its American Depositary Receipts trade on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker CAJ.

## Core Business Segments

Canon operates across several major business areas:

- **Imaging / Camera Systems** — Interchangeable-lens digital cameras (DSLR and mirrorless), EOS system, lenses, and broadcast/cinema optics. Canon has held the No. 1 share of the global interchangeable-lens digital camera market for 23 consecutive years (as of 2026).
- **Printing & Document Solutions** — Multifunction printers, laser printers (Satera series), inkjet printers, toner and ink cartridges, managed print services, and print security solutions. Canon is recognized as a "Leader" in the IDC MarketScape for Worldwide Print Security Solutions.
- **Medical Systems** — Diagnostic imaging equipment including CT scanners (Aquilion Rise), X-ray systems, and ultrasound. The Aquilion Rise CT scanner won the iF Gold Award at the 2026 iF Design Awards.
- **Industrial Equipment** — Semiconductor lithography equipment, including novel inkjet-based wafer planarization technology. Canon achieved a world first in 2026 with inkjet-based wafer surface planarization.
- **Network / Broadcast Cameras** — PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom) cameras for broadcast applications. In April 2026, Canon and Ross Video announced a partnership to deliver broadcast-grade PTZ solutions.

## Key Markets

Canon operates globally across Asia-Pacific, the Americas, Europe, and the Middle East/Africa. Key market activities include:

- **United States** — Major market; Canon U.S.A. is the North American subsidiary. Canon ranked seventh in the U.S. patent ranking in 2026, remaining in the top 10 for 42 consecutive years.
- **Europe** — Active intellectual property enforcement on e-commerce platforms (Amazon EU).
- **Asia** — Japan domestic market (Satera printer energy awards), Singapore (Lazada IP enforcement), China (JD.com IP enforcement).

## Canon U.S.A.

Canon U.S.A., Inc. is the North American subsidiary of Canon Inc., headquartered in Melville, NY. It is responsible for sales, marketing, and support of Canon's full product portfolio in the United States. Canon U.S.A. is pursuing a multifunction device remanufacturing business launch in the United States as part of a global rollout (announced March 2026).
